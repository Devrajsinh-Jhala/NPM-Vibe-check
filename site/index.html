honest security boundary

A strong checkpoint, not a magical shield.

npx-vibe is a pre-execution risk scanner—not a sandbox, antivirus product, or formal audit. It inspects bounded package content and can miss runtime-only, dependency, native-code, or remote-response behavior. Proceed is useful context, never proof of safety.

bring your own model

Pick a goal, not a model spreadsheet.

Stay deterministic, keep review local with Ollama, or choose fast, balanced, or strong. The exact resolved model is printed every time. Provider-specific environment variables keep keys out of shell history, new Gemini authorization keys are recognized, and ambiguous direct keys stop safely instead of being sent to a guessed service.
